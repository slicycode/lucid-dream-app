Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a dream?

Tap the + button on the Journal tab. Write your dream, add a title, select an emotion and themes, then tap Save. For best results, log your dream within 5 minutes of waking.

How does AI interpretation work?

After saving a dream, open it and tap "Interpret this dream." The AI analyzes your dream's symbols, emotions, and narrative to provide a personalized interpretation. Free users get 3 interpretations per week. Premium users get unlimited interpretations.

Is my dream data private?

Yes. All dreams are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server. The only time dream text leaves your device is when you tap "Interpret" — it's sent to an AI service for analysis, but is not stored there.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your device's Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → Lucid Premium → Cancel. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I delete all my data?

Simply delete the app from your device. All dreams, settings, and progress are stored locally and will be permanently removed when the app is uninstalled.

The AI interpretation isn't loading.

AI interpretation requires an internet connection. Check your connection and try again. If the issue persists, try force-closing and reopening the app.
